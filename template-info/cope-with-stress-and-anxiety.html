In contrast, an anxiety disorder involves a constant or very frequent feeling of irrational fear. This fear is intense and exhausting.

This type of anxiety can severely limit one's actions. In severe cases, it may prevent individuals from performing daily tasks such as entering an elevator, crossing the street, or even going outside. Without treatment, anxiety tends to worsen.

Anxiety disorders are the most common type of mental health disorder. They can affect anyone at any age. According to the American Psychiatric Association, women are more frequently diagnosed with anxiety disorders than men.
